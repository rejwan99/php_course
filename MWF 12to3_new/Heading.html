How many heading presents into the HTML ?

HTML defines six levels of headings, and these heading elements are H1, H2, H3, H4, H5, and H6. The H1 element is the highest level, and the H6 element is the least important. Any heading tag after h6 does not work. Example- h7, h8, h9, etc.
